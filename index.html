Cricket (Complete Subject)

Basics & Rules: Definition: Cricket ek bat-ball team game hai jo 11 players ke beech khela jata hai. Types: Test, ODI, T20. Example: India vs Australia Test Match. Details: Pitch 22 yards ka hota hai, runs score karke jeet hoti hai. History: England se shuru hua, ICC global body hai.
Major Tournaments: Definition: International & domestic level ke major cricket competitions. Types: ICC World Cup, T20 World Cup, Champions Trophy, Asia Cup, IPL. Example: ICC World Cup 2011 – India Winner. Details: Ye tournaments players ki ranking aur records decide karte hain.
Winners & Records: Definition: Tournament jeetne wali teams aur historical achievements. Includes: Men & Women World Cup Winners, IPL Winners, Asia Cup Winners. Example: Australia – most World Cups. Details: Cricket history ka statistical backbone.
Players & Records: Definition: Greatest cricketers aur unke records. Players: Sachin Tendulkar, Virat Kohli, MS Dhoni, Rohit Sharma, Steve Smith, Kane Williamson. Records: Most runs, wickets, centuries, hat-tricks. Example: Sachin – 100 international centuries.
Rules & Officials: Definition: Match chalane ke rules & decision makers. Topics: Overs, Innings, LBW, Powerplay, DRS, Umpires, Third Umpire. Example: DRS se decision review. Details: Fair play aur accuracy ke liye rules mandatory hain.
Grounds & Equipment: Definition: Cricket me use hone wale tools & field parts. Includes: Pitch, Boundary, Bat, Ball, Pads, Helmet. Example: Willow bat. Details: Safety aur performance ke liye equipment zaroori.
Stadiums: Definition: Cricket matches ke famous grounds. Examples: Lord’s, Eden Gardens, MCG, Wankhede, Adelaide Oval. Details: Stadiums cricket culture ka symbol hote hain.
Women & Domestic Cricket: Definition: Women aur domestic level ka cricket. Includes: Women’s World Cup, Ranji Trophy, Duleep Trophy, Vijay Hazare, Syed Mushtaq Ali. Example: Mithali Raj. Details: Talent development ka foundation.
Analytics & Strategy: Definition: Match planning & analysis. Types: Field placement, bowling strategy, batting techniques. Example: Powerplay field setting. Details: Modern cricket data-driven ho chuka hai.

Match Formats (Detailed):
Definition: Cricket alag-alag time duration aur rule set ke formats me khela jata hai.
Types: Test (5 days), ODI (50 overs), T20 (20 overs), T10, The Hundred.
Example: Ashes – Test Series.
Details: Format ke hisaab se strategy, stamina aur skills change hoti hain.
Ways of Dismissal:
Definition: Batsman ke out hone ke tarike.
Types: Bowled, Caught, LBW, Run Out, Stumped, Hit Wicket, Obstructing Field.
Example: Catch out at slips.
Details: Cricket ke rules ka sabse technical part.
Bowling Types:
Definition: Ball dalne ke alag-alag styles.
Types: Fast, Swing, Seam, Spin (Off-spin, Leg-spin), Yorkers, Bouncers.
Example: Shane Warne – Leg Spin.
Details: Bowling variation match ka result badal sakti hai.
